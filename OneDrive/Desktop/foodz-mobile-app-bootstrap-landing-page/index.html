Our Story

TREM is a dairy free, high protein, low sugar, ready to drink coffee. TREM is an acronym for Training Recovery Energy and Muscle Growth, TREM was created because we noticed that in the world today milk based sugar dense and calorie ready to drink coffee beverages flood the store shelves. Along on the store shelves are sugary energy drinks and chalky, high caloric, whey protein shakes. There is a multi billion dollar market for each of these three drink types and studies show that sales are expected to increase significantly. On the other hand the dairy free market is skyrocketing because consumers are becoming more health conscious. Consumers want foods and beverages that are healthy but still taste good. This is what inspired my team and I to create TREM.

TREM tastes similar to the established ready to drink coffee brands like Starbucks and Dunkin Donuts bottled frappuccinos but is way healthier and has the functionality of energy and protein drinks while remaining dairy free. TREM contains half of the sugar and calories as the leading brands of energy drinks and ready to drink coffees, contains the same amount of caffeine as energy drinks and has 15g of plant based protein. TREM is a multipurpose beverage. It can be consumed by athletes and gym goers for pre or post workout. It can be consumed by college students who need a caffeine jolt when studying for final exams, or by people who are health conscious and just simply like coffee. TREM is for everyone and can be used in various facets.
